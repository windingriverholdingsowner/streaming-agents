WHAT IS STEALTH STREAMING:
---------------------------
Stealth streaming = Broadcasting ambient, passive content (cozy rooms, burning candles, relaxing scenes) to multiple platforms simultaneously (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram) where products are displayed as part of the scene. The stream runs passively without requiring active hosting.

Benefits:
- Build watch time and followers passively
- Create relaxing content for viewers
- Showcase products in natural, cozy settings
- Monetize through ads/gifts with minimal effort
- Multi-platform reach

EQUIPMENT NEEDED:
-----------------
- Camera/Phone with good view of products
- Stable mount/tripod
- Good lighting to showcase product designs and colors
- Computer or phone capable of running streaming software
- Reliable internet connection

STREAMING SOFTWARE OPTIONS:
---------------------------
Multi-Platform:
- Restream.io (free tier available)
- StreamYard (browser-based, user-friendly)
- OBS Studio (free, powerful, more complex)

Mobile Apps:
- Prism Live Studio
- Streamlabs Mobile

SETUP APPROACH:
---------------
1. Use AI to generate cozy room backgrounds
2. Composite thePUNShopTeesNGifts products into the scenes
3. Create slideshow or video loop
4. Stream to multiple platforms simultaneously

AI TOOLS RECOMMENDED:
---------------------
Image Generation:
- Midjourney (excellent for photorealistic interiors)
- DALL-E 3 (accessible via ChatGPT Plus)
- Leonardo.ai (free tier available)
- Stable Diffusion (free but requires setup)

Video/Animation:
- Runway ML (create ambient video from images)
- Pika Labs (turn AI images into moving scenes)
- Kaiber (creates looping ambient videos)

Photo Editing/Compositing:
- Photoshop (or free alternatives: GIMP, Photopea)
- Canva Pro (background remover + layering)
- Runway's inpainting (AI-powered blending)

4. Set up slideshow or looping video
